Hello all, its been a really long time since I put down my thoughts. I was travelling to a lot of places and couldn't find time to share all those experiences (but, now I am here. Be ready for the spam 😉). Firstly, this winter was amazing for me with a lot of variety in destinations, journey and people. I would like to start with the Pondicherry experience as I was postponing going there since my first year (for a very different reason) and I finally went there.

There is this ritual among our class 10 friends that we should definitely meet during every semester and that was happening really well but everytime we meet in Vijayawada. I (and prolly most of us) wanted a change from visiting PVP or Trendset and watching a movie, followed by lunch and then sharing life updates. So we decided to go somewhere else and we decided that to be Pondi during New Year. Everyone was super excited and I was the most excited ig, cause I have been waiting so badly to visit Pondi and now that is happening with my closest buddies from school (Chaitanya ditched in the last minute tho 🙃).

We started from Vijayawada by bus and reached Pondi by Dec 31st morning. I have this habit of going without planning anything and as usual the same happened (except for the stay). We went to the stay and got freshen up, took scooty for rental and then started to the most famous places of pondi (cosy cafes), starting with `Anthe Cafe` we visited a bunch of other cafes along the beach (I forgot the name of the area, prolly rock beach ig) and then we roamed and started talking about life and ~~about girls (you know how a boys trip works).~~ This was a lot of fun cause we got to re-live the moments we experienced in school where we talk about random shit and everything on the earth starting from big bang to the end of the world.

I think the first time pondi experience is worth the delay. I would love to visit the place some other time again.
